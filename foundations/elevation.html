Elevation (shadow)

Four levels of depth. No glow variants — elevation only. Each level maps to a concrete component category.

shadow-xs
--ins-shadow-xs · borders, inline rows
shadow-sm
--ins-shadow-sm · cards, navbar
shadow-md
--ins-shadow-md · tooltips, floating panels
shadow-lg
--ins-shadow-lg · modals, dropdowns, menus
