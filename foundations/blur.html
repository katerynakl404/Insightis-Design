Blur

Backdrop-filter values for glass surfaces (navbar, modal scrim). Always pair with a solid fallback bg for browsers without backdrop-filter support.

blur-4
--ins-blur-4 · subtle
blur-8
--ins-blur-8 · navbar glass (default)
blur-16
--ins-blur-16 · modal scrim
blur-24
--ins-blur-24 · hero dim

Safe usage

.ins-navbar {
  background: var(--ins-surface-container);   /* fallback solid */
  background: color-mix(in srgb, var(--ins-surface-container) 72%, transparent);
  backdrop-filter: blur(var(--ins-blur-8));
  -webkit-backdrop-filter: blur(var(--ins-blur-8));
}
