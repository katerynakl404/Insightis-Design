Radius

Seven rounding steps from 2px to full pill. Semantic aliases for buttons, cards, badges, inputs.

Scale

radius-2 (xs) — badges, indicators
radius-4 (sm) — checkbox, tag
radius-6 (md) — small input
radius-8 (lg) — input, avatar
radius-12 (xl) — card, panel
radius-16 (2xl) — modal, hero
radius-20 (3xl) — drawer
radius-full (pill) — button, nav
